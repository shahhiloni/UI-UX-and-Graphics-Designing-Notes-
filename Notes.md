--------------------------------- What is UI/UX -----------------------------
- UI (User Interface)

Definition:
UI is the visual design and layout of a website or mobile app that users see and interact with,
such as buttons, colors, icons, menus, and screens.

- UX (User Experience)

Definition:
UX is the overall experience and ease of use a user feels while using a website or app, including navigation, comfort, speed, and problem-solving.

UI/UX design is the process of creating digital products that are visually attractive, easy to use, and provide a smooth user experience.

------------------------ What is Graphic Designing --------------------------
- Graphic Designing is the creative process of combining images, colors, typography, shapes, and layout to communicate a message or idea visually.

- It is used to create logos, posters, advertisements, social media posts, websites, and branding materials that attract attention and convey information clearly and effectively.
